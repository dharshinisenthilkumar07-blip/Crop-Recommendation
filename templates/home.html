About Agriculture

Agriculture feeds the world. It involves the cultivation of soil, growing crops, and raising livestock. Modern practices now use data and AI to improve yields and conserve resources.

Agriculture in India

India is among the largest producers of rice, wheat, and spices. Agriculture is a livelihood for over half the population. Improving technology and training can uplift rural communities.

Sustainable Farming

Sustainable agriculture focuses on long-term productivity without harming the environment. Practices include crop rotation, composting, and reduced chemical use.

Soil Health Matters

Understanding soil nutrients (NPK), pH, and moisture is key to crop success. Our tool helps analyze these factors to recommend the best crops for your land.
